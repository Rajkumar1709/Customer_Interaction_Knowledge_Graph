# User Stories & Workflows

## Primary User
**Customer Success Manager (CSM) / Account Representative** and **Support Team Member**.

## Primary User Story
> “As a CSM, when I open an account, I want to instantly see all related issues, interactions, and risk indicators in one place, so I can understand what is happening and take proactive action before renewal or escalation.”

## The Workflow Transformation
Currently, Customer Health Scores (CHS) use static variables. We aim to transition to a more accurate, insight-driven framework leveraging the knowledge graph.

### 1. Financial Health
Adding predictive financial risk indicators to strengthen early warning capabilities:
- Renewal risk, AR aging, past-due balance, payment consistency.
- Billing disputes.

### 2. Engagement & Relationship Health
Reflects the strength, risk, stability, and sentiment of the relationship:
- Engagement quality (depth, sentiment, CSAT, meetings).
- Portfolio-level dynamics (exec turnover, tech consolidation).

### 3. Support Health
Replacing raw case counts with weighted, severity-based indicators of support friction:
- Weighted PME scoring (P1/P2/P3).
- PME severity, aging, and trends.
- Percentage of portfolio affected by support issues.

### 4. Product Health
Deep dive on product usage volume, adoption, value realization, and performance:
- Feature adoption.
- Product stability and performance.
- Usage quality.
