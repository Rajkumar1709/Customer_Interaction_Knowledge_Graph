# RealPage Customer Interaction Knowledge Graph

Welcome to the documentation for the Customer Interaction Knowledge Graph project, sponsored by **Titina Ott-Adams**, built during the Hackathon to solve critical customer success and support visibility challenges.

## Goals and Motivation

**Challenge Name:** Customer Interaction Knowledge Graph  
**Core System:** Support, Customer Success, Sales, Implementations and Product  

### Problem Statement
Currently, it is hard to find information due to the disparity between systems (Salesforce, OMS, Smartsheet, UDM). Inconsistent use of Salesforce across teams leads to a lack of a single source of truth.

Our goal is to build a dynamic knowledge graph of customer interactions — linking:
- Tickets
- Calls
- Products
- Properties
- Implementations
- Escalations
- Renewals
- Revenue Impact 

This intelligence layer allows us to identify patterns across customers, surface hidden dependencies, detect systemic issues, and provide context-aware recommendations to Sales, CS, and Product teams.

### Business Impact
This directly aligns with **Project Next: Product Reliability and Core Experience**.
Specifically, the solution aims to:
- Reduce PME noise by reducing PME backlog by **5%**
- Drive stability and performance with a **10%** improvement in Mean Time To Resolve (MTTR)

## Unique Angle: Beyond Just a Graph
"Not just a graph — an explainable customer risk engine"

While many graph demos stop at visualization, this project goes further:
- **Identifies Risk**: Using AI logic on structured graph signals.
- **Explains Risk**: Generating root-cause narratives.
- **Recommends Actions**: Giving CSMs actionable next steps. 

Furthermore, we allow **Human Context Overlays**. An AI handles the structured signals, while a CSM can add qualitative signals (e.g. "Leadership change at customer"), drastically improving the quality of the decisions.
