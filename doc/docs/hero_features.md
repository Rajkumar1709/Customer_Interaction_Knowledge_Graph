# Hero Features

The Intelligence Layer provides a dashboard or GPT/Agent that filters by PMC (Property Management Company) and can answer critical questions with actionable insights.

## Agent Capabilities

The system answers the following questions intelligently:

### What are the risks?
- Factors in backlog and aging.
- Analyzes number of tickets and resolution times outside of SLA.
- Identifies the length of time since last contact.

### What are the opportunities?
- Identifies current solutions with competitors.
- Uncovers explicit Customer Goals.

### What is the customer sentiment?
- Evaluates customer stickiness metrics.
- Considers major initiative adoption (e.g., Unified migration, new OneSite).
- Checks ratio of "Many vs. Few" products, last contact date, and recent billing issues.

### What are the support case trends?
- Identifies types of cases, problematic products, and ticket aging metrics.

### What is the status of the open backlog?
- Checks aging, out of SLA cases.
- Provides a summary of recently implemented orders with insights into customizations.

### Who are the active customer contacts?
- Retrieves contact recency.
- Answers "How long since the last support case/communication?".

## Core Data Views (Dashboard)
- **Implementation Orders (PMC):** Open Orders Backlog with products, status, and aging.
- **Support:** Open cases and PME (Problem Management Escalations).
- **Billing:** Customer Activity at the PMC Account Level (Excessive Aging).
- **Major Initiative Status:** Unified migration, new OneSite, Fee Transparency, MFA integrations.
- **Relationship History (Nice to Have):** Mapping the contact's history (New to PMC/RP?).
